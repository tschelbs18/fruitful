Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Fruitful?

A: Fruitful is a productivity tool to help people stay organized and motivated while going through their daily tasks. You can create a list of the things you want to accomplish, and earn rewards and points you set for yourself.

Q: How do I get started with Fruitful?

A: First, register an account if you are a new user, or if you already have an account login here.
Next, add some tasks to your daily to-do list and tell us how big of a lift that task will be. If it's easy, call it a grape. If it's a handful, call it an apple. If it's an armload, call it a watermelon. Don't worry, we'll reward you accordingly.
Then, add some rewards to stay motivated. If you do the work of harvesting, you should be able to enjoy the fruits of your labor (get it?). Grapes, apples, and watermelons are rewards for easy, medium, and hard tasks respectively. When you complete a task, you'll earn points which you can then redeem for rewards. We've filled out some basic options for rewards and you can add personalized ones for yourself to stay motivated.
Last, go ahead and enjoy your rewards or take a look at the scoreboard for all-time or daily productivity to see how you stack up. Remember, the points don't matter and we hope you have a fruitful day!

Q: I found an error or I need help, how do I get support?

A: Submit an error report. We'll handle it as soon as we can.

Q: How do I change my profile information?

A: Self-service password changes and other profile information pages are currently in development. For now, submit an error report and we'll handle whatever you need on our end.
